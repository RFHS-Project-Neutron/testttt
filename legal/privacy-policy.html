Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 12, 2024

Project Neutron ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website https://project-neutron.site. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about users ("you") of our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data such as names, email addresses, or other identifying information. However, we use third-party tools to collect and process anonymous user data for website analytics and advertising purposes.

  • Google Analytics: Tracks user interactions, session data, and anonymized demographics.
  • Microsoft Clarity: Tracks user sessions and behavior to provide insights on site usage.
  • Cloudflare: Collects anonymized data to enhance website security and performance.
  • Monetag: Used for displaying ads and may collect anonymized data for targeted advertising.

2. Open-Source Games

All games available on Project Neutron are open-source and sourced from GitHub repositories. These games comply with open-source licenses, ensuring that they are legal for use, modification, and distribution according to their respective licenses.

3. Movies and TV Shows

The movies and TV shows displayed on Project Neutron are not hosted by us. We do not store or control this media content. All media is streamed from third-party hosting providers, and we are not responsible for the availability, legality, or content of the media provided by these services.

4. How We Use Collected Data

We use data collected through analytics and advertising services for the following purposes:

  • To understand and improve user experience on our site.
  • To analyze site performance and optimize game features.
  • To display relevant ads through third-party networks (Monetag).
  • To enhance site security using Cloudflare’s services.

5. Sharing of Data

We may share anonymized data with:

  • Monetag: For advertising purposes.
  • DuckHTML: For collaborative analytics and performance improvement.
  • Third-party service providers: As part of analytics and security tools (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, Cloudflare).

6. International Data Transfers

Our users span across more than 20 countries. We ensure that any data transferred to third-party services complies with international data protection regulations.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to track user interactions on our site:

  • Essential Cookies: Used for site functionality.
  • Performance Cookies: Used by Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Monetag.

8. User Rights

You can opt out of Google Analytics by using this browser add-on and manage ad personalization settings via Monetag or your browser’s privacy settings.

9. Security

We use Cloudflare to help protect our website from unauthorized access and ensure secure data transmission.

10. Children’s Privacy

We do not have age restrictions on our site, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page.
